=================
3. In Elvindore
=================

..
    Berya arrives at Emansun and sneaks into Asya’s bedroom. She tells her
    Kro’s injured. Asya worries, and calls Raikhon and Karzon. Raikhon
    remembers that there are Khaay in the city, and he summons them for their
    help.

    Asya and the Khaay talk as they ride to Elvindore with Berya, Raikhon and
    the others. They reach the village, and the Khaay immediately go to Kro.
    Gorto heals Kro. Kro goes into a slight coma, but he is well.
